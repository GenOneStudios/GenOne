ABOUT US

Gen One Studios is made up of a group of friends who met at California State Polytechnic University, Pomona. We bonded together over a shared love of games and music, and dedicated to put those passions to work. We don't just want to make games, we waant to create experiences. To draw the player into our stories, worlds, and chellenges, we aim to marry narrative and gameplay ideas. Our first title, Travelers, is currently under development.

Travelers

Action RPG | PC

Travelers is an action platformer that takes as many inspirations from bullet-hells as it does from the genre staples. Tight movement mechanics and a variety of abilities are your tools to take on "The Underneath" as its denizens do everything they can to challenge you. Can you brave its depths and uncover the secrets of those that went before? Read More
